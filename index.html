Fiat Lux — Experimental Podcast Series

Fiat Lux is a podcast series experimenting with sounds, format, and delivery, offering something unexpected every episode. Each episode creates a listening experience that defies convention. Whether you're here for eclectic tunes or mysterious narratives, this series invites you to dive into the unknown and be surprised by creative audio expression. Find the episodes 001 and 002 here.

  1. Fiat Lux - 003

    Need a dose of eclectic tunes? Presenting the podcast series Fiat Lux, where I'll be sharing those extra tracks that didn't quite fit anywhere else. From synth melodies to experimental beats, join me on this musical journey through 2020s, and possibly beyond! I'll probably always have odd extras, which will guarantee the longevity of this podcast.

  8. Fiat Lux - 010

    This episode is both experimental and transitional, blending creativity and reflection in unexpected ways. It includes a captivating short story, an anonymous message to the world, and an entry from Burning Man 2024 featuring contributions from Wayne DeFehr, Danica Hoffart, Trevor DePagie, and LR Friberg. You'll also hear unreleased music by Pete Swinton alongside tracks from Black Saturn and Jon Shuemaker. And, as always, expect to encounter at least two synthetic voices throughout the experience.
